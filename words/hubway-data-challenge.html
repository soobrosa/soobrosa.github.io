Hubway Data Challenge

> 12 DEC 2012 :: soobrosa

Applied for Hubway Data Challenge with Balázs Krich.

Learned:

  • Tilemill is good, but you need time and a plan,
  • titles are major on maps,
  • USGS NED and (ASTER)[http://gdem.ersdac.jspacesystems.or.jp/search.jsp] are geil if you go for topographic maps,
  • if your data has both timestamp and geocode go Torque to animate them. Some Google Directions API magic helps.



Anyway this way a real ramp up in digital mapping.
